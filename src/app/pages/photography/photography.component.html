About

A lens on
the world

Photography is how I slow down. Between lines of code and architectural decisions, the camera is my reset — a way of seeing the world with fresh eyes and unhurried attention.

I'm drawn to the quiet drama of landscapes at golden hour, the humbling scale of a star-filled sky, the energy of a crowded street, and the story written on a person's face.

Every frame is a question: what am I really looking at?
